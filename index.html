An online card game of human vs machine

It’s the year 2100 and robots have taken over the world. Humanity has all but disapeared. You are the leader of the last surviving group of humans. Will a combination of strategy and chance cause you to thrive during these hard times and grow your people into a powerful multitude, or will you be overcome by the robots?

How to play Dead Humanity

You and the robots each get 6 cards

Take turns playing one card each round

In Dead Humanity, your civilization begins with 100 population, 200 food and 10 soldiers. Each card will alter the population, soldiers or food for you or your opponent, as will be explained on the card. You will also receive 2 more food for every new person, and 1 new soldier for every 10 new people. But beware, your scores will also drop as your people die.

At round 10 and 20, you’ll need to choose between declaring war or making peace

Choosing war requires you have more soldiers than your enemy. Declaring war will allow each of your soldiers to kill 3 people from your enemy's population.

Choosing peace requires you have at least 200 food. Making peace will let your civilization grow by 50% immediately.

The card game ends if your population, soldier count or food reaches zero. Humanity is dead...all of civilization is over. If you both survive to round 20, the winner will be the player with the greatest population.
